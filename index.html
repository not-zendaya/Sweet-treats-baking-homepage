Chocolate Lava Cake

A decadent dessert with a molten chocolate center tha flows like lava when you cut into it. Perfect for special occasions or when you need a chocolate fix!

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup (115g) butter
  • 4 oz (113g) dark chocolate
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup (50g) sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Pinch of salt

Tip:Bake at 425°F for 12-14 minutes until edges are set but center is still soft!
